Coursework

Spring Quarter 2021 at bellevue College


PROG 109 | Adam Brehm

Introduction to Web Development • 5 Cr
Introduces fundamental programming techniques using current web development software. Students design procedures and write computer instructions to solve business problems, learn procedural programming, develop graphical user interfaces for the web and work with events and objects.

What did you know before this class?

Moderate knowledge of programming fundamentals, minimal understanding of html, and self-taught understanding of GitHub and WordPress.

What knowledge/skills did you gain after this class?

Fundamental and much more complete understanding of html, javascript, css, and web development practices.



POLS 250 | David Spataro

Environmental Politics • 5 Cr
Does nature have political rights? What are the best policies to guarantee justice for all people amidst global warming and climate disruption? Why do marginalized groups face a disproportionate amount of environmental toxins? This class examines the relationship between political and environmental systems. Students will learn about the history of environmental politics and examine key debates concerning environmental policy and civic action.

What did you know before this class?

General political awareness of the issues at play and the state of the environment and climate.

What knowledge/skills did you gain after this class?

A much deeper and more specific understanding of the history of environmental politics and the debates within it as they are today.
