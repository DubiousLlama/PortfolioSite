Ashen Lands TTRPG

My main passion project right now is Ashen Lands, a tabletop roleplaying game (like Dungeons and Dragons) I'm currently designing.

You can check out the project at ashenlands.seamusallen.com, and follow for updates at twitter.com/AshenLandsRPG.

The game is set in the wake of a supernatural apocalypse, and this is its premise:

Perhaps you lived it yourself, or you are young enough to have only heard the tales of those who did: on that fateful day, three decades ago, some ill magic slouched across the world, itâ€™s disinterested malevolence seeping in from every shadowed corner and unwatched crevice.

That day, space itself seemed to contort and twist, as though writhing in pain. Volcanoes erupted and colossal crevasses opened, great demon-armies spilling forth from the bowels of the earth, taking no prisoners as their fires swept the landscape. Storms scoured the world. What it did not destroy, it twisted: reality-warping eldritch creatures emerged from everyday animals and objects, mutating into terrifying abominations, and despite no sorcerer conjuring them, the dead walked again.

To this day, no one has found the face, the name, the source of the evil that cast its shadow over this now-forsaken land, that patiently awaits the passing of this dying world.

Little remains.

Unspeakable terrors stalk the landscape, and death is always imminent. Scattered bands of people survive amidst the ruins of once-great empires, and what suffering life still exists is fading.

Yet there is hope. There is always hope. A small band of heroes gather. Will you be a spark of light in the darkness, or yet another dream undone?
